About Tagudin Mobile Clinic and Wellness Center

Tagudin Mobile Clinic and Wellness Center is committed to delivering accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare services to individuals and families in Tagudin, Ilocos Sur, and neighboring communities. Our goal is to provide patient-centered care through modern medical facilities, reliable diagnostic services, and compassionate healthcare professionals.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable, and compassionate healthcare services by combining skilled healthcare professionals, modern medical technology, and patient-centered care for every member of our community.

Our Vision

To become one of the most trusted community healthcare providers in Ilocos Sur, recognized for excellence in medical services, innovation, and genuine compassion for every patient.

Our Story

Tagudin Mobile Clinic and Wellness Center was established with the goal of making quality healthcare more accessible to the people of Tagudin and nearby municipalities. From consultation and laboratory services to diagnostic imaging and wellness programs, our clinic continues to grow while maintaining a commitment to compassionate, ethical, and professional patient care.
