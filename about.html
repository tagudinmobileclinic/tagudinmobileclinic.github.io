About Our Clinic

Providing quality healthcare with compassion and excellence.

Our Story

Tagudin Mobile Clinic and Wellness Center (MCWC) is committed to providing quality, affordable, and compassionate healthcare services to the people of Tagudin, Ilocos Sur, and neighboring communities. Since its establishment, the clinic has continuously improved its medical facilities and healthcare services while remaining faithful to its mission of serving every patient with excellence and compassion.

Better Health, Better Soul

Our Vision

MCWC envisions to provide an excellent healing physically, mentally and spiritually.

Our Objectives

• Fast, reliable and complete diagnostics
• Competent specialty services
• Courteous and hospitable personnel creating a compassionate and loving environment to our constituents
• Prayer and counseling to those who are in need
• Outreaching communities in need (jail, medical missions, community teaching)
• Financial support to Christian churches consistent with the Christian principles on which we were founded

Committed to Better Healthcare

Our clinic combines experienced healthcare professionals, modern diagnostic facilities, and personalized patient care to ensure every individual receives the attention and treatment they deserve.

We continuously strive to improve our services while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, safety, and compassion.
