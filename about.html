About Our Clinic

Providing quality healthcare with compassion and excellence.

Our Story

Tagudin Mobile Clinic and Wellness Center was established with a vision of making quality healthcare more accessible to every family in Tagudin and nearby communities. Through dedication, innovation, and compassionate service, we continue to provide trusted medical care for patients of all ages.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide affordable, accessible, and quality healthcare through compassionate service, modern medical technology, and highly competent healthcare professionals.

👁️

Our Vision

To become one of the most trusted healthcare providers in Ilocos Sur by delivering excellent, patient-centered medical services.

❤️

Our Core Values

Compassion, Integrity, Excellence, Respect, Teamwork, and Commitment to every patient we serve.

Committed to Better Healthcare

Our clinic combines experienced healthcare professionals, modern diagnostic facilities, and personalized patient care to ensure every individual receives the attention and treatment they deserve.

We continuously strive to improve our services while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, safety, and compassion.
