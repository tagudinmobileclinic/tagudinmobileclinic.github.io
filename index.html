About Us

Tagudin Mobile Clinic and Wellness Center is committed to providing quality, affordable, and compassionate healthcare services to individuals and families in Tagudin, Ilocos Sur, and nearby communities. Our mission is to deliver accessible medical care through our clinic and mobile healthcare services while promoting wellness and disease prevention.
